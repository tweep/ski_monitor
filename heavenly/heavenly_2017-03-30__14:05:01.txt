</div><div class="page-body">

<h3>Updated throughout the day, below you’ll find the current status for all lifts and runs on the mountain.&nbsp; The Twitter feed (<a href="https://twitter.com/HVconditions" target="_blank">@HVConditions</a>) on the right will give you up-to-the-minute info from around the mountain.</h3>
<h3><span>For text updates throughout the day text the word “HEAVENLY” to 99-000.&nbsp; You’ll receive daily info on lift operations, terrain, snow totals and parking. You’ll always know what's going on here at Heavenly!</span></h3>
<p><span><br>
</span></p>
<h4>Lift status that is set as “Open” prior to 9:00 a.m. Mon-Fri and 8:30 a.m. Sat-Sun indicates which lifts are SCHEDULED to open for the day, weather permitting. Due to changing weather, scheduled lifts may change to “Hold” or “Closed” status upon opening.</h4>
